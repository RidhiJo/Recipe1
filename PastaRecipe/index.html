Pesto Pasta with Basil, Cherry tomatoes and Mozzarella

By: Chef Ridhi Joshi

Serves: 2

Pesto Pasta is easy to make and can be had as a snacks or light meal for kids. It takes just 15 minutes to prepare it.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of boiled pasta. Any type of pasta: penne, macaroni or fussili.
  • 1 small cup of green peas.
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil.
  • 6 cherry tomatoes.
  • 2 tablespoon pesto sauce.
  • Salt to taste.
  • 1 small cup of grated Mozarella

Method

  1. Heat 1 tablespoon of olive oil in a pan.
  2. Chop the cherry tomatoes.
  3. Add the chopped tomatoes to the frying pan.
  4. Add 1 small cup of green peas to the frying pan. Stir it well.
  5. Add 2 tablespoon pesto sauce. Stir it to mix the sauce well with the tomatoes and peas.
  6. Add 1 cup of boiled pasta to the frying pan.
  7. Add salt to taste.
  8. Stir it well
  9. Add 1 small cup of grated mozarella to garnish.
